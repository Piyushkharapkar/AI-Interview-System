How It Works

MockMate-AI is your personal AI-driven interview preparation tool. Follow the steps below to get started:

Step 1: Start a New Interview

Click on the "+ Add New" button on your dashboard to begin a new mock interview.

Step 2: Enter Job Details

Fill in your job role, tech stack, and years of experience in the form.

Step 3: Begin Interview

Click the "Start Interview" button, and the questions will be given according to your input.

Step 4: Answer Questions

Respond to the generated questions just like a real interview.

Step 5: Get Feedback

Receive instant feedback to improve your responses and interview skills.
